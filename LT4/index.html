WISHING STARS

            

I still search

            for you in crowds,

            in empty fields

            and soaring clouds.

In city lights

            and passing cars,

            on winding roads

            and wishing stars.

I wonder where

            you could be now,

            for years i've not said

            your name out load.

And longer since                        

I called you mine----      

time has passed              

for you and I.                    

But I have learnt                         

to live without,                

I do not mind---                

I still love you anyhow.

                             

                                                                                     --- Lang Laev
